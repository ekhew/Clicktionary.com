Zealios V2
*

Quick Information

The Zealios V2 is a tactile switch with one of the most tactile bumps. You can choose from different weights when buying this switch.

Specialties

  • Tactile bump near the top of keypress
  • Very little to no wobble
  • Bigger, smoother bump compared to Holy Pandas

Specifications

Colors clear housing, purple stem
Clicky No
Tactile Yes
Travel ~ mm actuation, ~ mm bottom out
Weight 62g, 65g, 67g, 78g bottom out
Life Span ~ million presses
