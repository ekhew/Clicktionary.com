Holy Panda

Quick Information

The Holy Panda switch is a tactile switch made using the stems from the Holy Clear or the Halo True, and the housing of the Invyr Panda switch. This is one of the most sought after tactile switches in the community.

Specialties

  • Tactile bump near middle of keypress

Specifications

  • Colors: cream housing, salmon stem
  • Clicky: No
  • Tactile: Yes
  • Key Travel: ~ mm actuation, ~ mm bottom out
  • Weight: 67g bottom out
  • Life Span: ~ presses
