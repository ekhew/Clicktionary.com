Cherry MX Black

Quick Information

The Cherry MX Black switch is a common linear switch when it comes to off the self keyboards.

Specialties

  • Common in off the shelf boards
  • Heavier than Cherry MX Reds

Specifications

  • Colors: black housing, black stem
  • Clicky: No
  • Tactile: No
  • Key Travel: 2.0mm actuation, 4.0mm bottom out
  • Weight: 60g bottom out
  • Life Span: ~50 million presses

Where to Find

  • amazon.com
  • mechanicalkeyboards.com
