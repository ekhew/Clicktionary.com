Kailh BOX Jade
*

Quick Information

The Kailh BOX Jade is a clicky switch produced by Kaihua. This switch got its name from the box that encases the top part of the stem.

Specialties

  • Waterproof
  • THICK clicks

Specifications

Colors white/clear housing, light green stem
Clicky Yes
Tactile Yes
Travel 3.6mm bottom out
Weight 50g actuation, 60g bottom out
Life Span ~50 million presses
