Kailh BOX Navy

Quick Information

The Kailh BOX Navy is a clicky switch produced by Kaihua. This switch got its name from the box that encases the top part of stem.

Specialties

  • Waterproof
  • THICK clicks

Specifications

  • Colors: white/clear housing, navy stem
  • Clicky: Yes
  • Tactile: Yes
  • Key Travel: 3.6mm bottom out
  • Weight: 60g actuation, 90g bottom out
  • Life Span: ~50 million presses
