Cherry MX Blue

Quick Information

The Cherry MX blue switch is the most common clicky switch when it comes to off the self keyboards. This switch is your standard most standard clicky switch.

Specialties

  • Common in off the shelf boards

Specifications

  • Colors: black housing, blue stem
  • Clicky: Yes
  • Tactile: Yes
  • Key Travel: 2.0mm actuation, 4.0mm bottom out
  • Weight: 50g actuation, 60g bottom out
  • Life Span: ~50 million presses
